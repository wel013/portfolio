Project 2a: The Walking Deadline

Overview

The project simulates a zombie battle where the player, equipped with a quiver of arrows, faces rounds of zombie attacks. The player must strategically shoot zombies, considering their movement speed and distance, prioritizing those with the lowest ETA managed by using a priority queue.

Key Components

  • Simulation of Battle:
    • Detailing actions within each round, including the strategic shooting of zombies based on movement speed and distance. The priority queue prioritizes zombies with the lowest ETA, contributing to the game's challenging dynamics.

Achievements

  • Successfully implemented and integrated various priority queues, enhancing the efficiency of zombie movement processing.
  • Successfully executed the rescue mission, generating visualized maps or coordinate lists based on user selections.

Technologies & Skills:

C++, Priority Queues, Inheritance, Dynamic Polymorphism
